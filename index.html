The Avara Museum is an immersive media art exhibition hall presented by the world-class digital design company District. Since the first Art Museum was introduced in Aewol, Jeju in late September 2020, District has been producing new exhibition works under the theme of Eternal NATURE and spreading the Avara Museum to various regions at home and abroad. Visit the Art Museum, which is composed of differentiated concepts and works, using each region's natural and cultural heritage.
